Routine 2: Cardio and Conditioning

Goal: Improve endurance and cardiovascular fitness

Frequency: 3 times a week (e.g., Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday)

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

Dynamic Stretching: High knees, butt kicks, arm swings (5 minutes)

Light Jog: Easy pace to gradually increase heart rate (5 minutes) Workout (45 minutes)

Interval Sprints

20 minutes: Alternate between 30 seconds of sprinting and 1 minute of walking/jogging Cool down for 2 minutes walking after finishing.

Circuit Training (Repeat 3 times)

Burpees: 10 reps Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds Jump Squats: 10 reps Push-Ups: 10-15 reps Rest: 1 minute between circuits

Steady-State Cardio

15 minutes of cycling, rowing, or jogging at a moderate pace Cool Down (10 minutes)

Static Stretching

:

Focus on legs, arms, and back (hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds)
