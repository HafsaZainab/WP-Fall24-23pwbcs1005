Routine 1: Full-Body Strength Training

Goal: Build muscle and strength Frequency: 3 times a week (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

Dynamic Stretching: Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists (5 minutes)

Light Cardio: Jogging in place or jumping jacks (5 minutes)

Workout (60 minutes)

Squats 4 sets of 8-10 reps Rest 60 seconds between sets

Bench Press (or Push-Ups)

4 sets of 8-10 reps Rest 60 seconds between sets

Deadlifts

4 sets of 8-10 reps Rest 60 seconds between sets

Bent-Over Rows

3 sets of 10-12 reps Rest 60 seconds between sets

Plank

3 sets of 30-60 seconds Rest 30 seconds between sets

Lunges

3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg Rest 60 seconds between sets Cool Down (10 minutes)

Static Stretching

:

Focus on major muscle groups (hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds)
